## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Primary Uses

- **Provide the Service:** Enable case posting, tip submission, and bounty payments
- **Match Cases:** Help connect finders with cases in their area
- **Process Payments:** Facilitate bounty payments and platform fees
- **Verify Users:** Confirm identity for poster and law enforcement accounts
- **Communicate:** Send notifications, updates, and important information
- **Improve Service:** Analyze usage to enhance features and user experience

### 3.2 Specific Uses

**For Finders:**
- Show relevant cases based on your location
- Notify you of new cases in your area
- Track your submissions and earnings
- Process bounty payments

**For Posters:**
- Display your cases to potential finders
- Notify you of new submissions
- Help verify submissions
- Process bounty payments

**For Law Enforcement:**
- Verify agency credentials
- Coordinate wanted criminal cases
- Facilitate information sharing

### 3.3 Legal and Safety Uses

- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect against fraud and abuse
- Ensure user safety
- Cooperate with law enforcement
- Enforce our Terms of Service

## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 Active Accounts

- Account data: Retained while account is active
- Case data: Retained while case is active plus 1 year
- Submission data: Retained for 2 years
- Payment data: Retained for 7 years (tax/legal requirements)

### 5.2 Deleted Accounts

- Most data deleted within 30 days
- Some data retained for legal/fraud prevention (anonymized when possible)
- Payment records retained for 7 years
- Law enforcement cases may be retained longer

### 5.3 Resolved Cases

- Resolved cases archived after 1 year
- May be deleted after 5 years
- Statistical data may be retained indefinitely (anonymized)
