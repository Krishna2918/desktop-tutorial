# Community Guidelines - I Found!!

**Last Updated:** November 6, 2025

## Welcome to I Found!!

Our mission is to help reunite families, recover lost items, and assist law enforcement through the power of community. These guidelines ensure a safe, respectful, and effective platform for everyone.

---

## Core Principles

### 1. **Safety First**
Your safety and the safety of others is our top priority.

### 2. **Truth and Accuracy**
All information must be truthful, accurate, and verified to the best of your ability.

### 3. **Respect and Empathy**
Treat all users with respect, especially those experiencing loss or tragedy.

### 4. **Legal Compliance**
Follow all local, state, and federal laws.

### 5. **Platform Integrity**
Help us maintain trust by reporting violations and fraudulent activity.

## For Case Posters

### Posting Missing Persons Cases

**‚úÖ DO:**

- **Provide Accurate Information:** Include truthful, detailed descriptions
- **Use Recent Photos:** Post clear, recent photos of the missing person
- **File Police Reports:** Report to law enforcement first, then post here
- **Update Status:** Mark cases as resolved when found
- **Respect Privacy:** Share only information that helps with the search
- **Set Reasonable Bounties:** Offer fair rewards within your means
- **Respond to Tips:** Review and respond to submissions promptly
- **Show Gratitude:** Thank those who help, regardless of outcome

**‚ùå DON'T:**

- **Post Without Authority:** Only family, legal guardians, or law enforcement can post
- **Withhold Critical Info:** Don't hide important details that could help
- **Exaggerate:** Don't embellish details to attract more attention
- **Abandon Cases:** Keep cases updated and respond to tips
- **Share Too Much:** Don't include sensitive information (SSN, medical records)
- **Discriminate in Verification:** Verify tips fairly based on merit

### Posting Lost Items Cases

**‚úÖ DO:**

- **Describe Accurately:** Provide detailed, accurate descriptions
- **Include Photos:** Show multiple angles and identifying features
- **Specify Location:** Indicate where item was lost
- **Set Fair Bounties:** Offer rewards proportional to item value
- **Verify Ownership:** Be prepared to prove ownership
- **Mark as Found:** Update case when item is recovered

**‚ùå DON'T:**

- **Post for Items You Don't Own:** Only post for your own property
- **Inflate Value:** Don't exaggerate item value
- **Refuse Reasonable Verification:** Accept legitimate proof when item is found
- **Avoid Paying Bounties:** Honor bounty commitments

### For Law Enforcement Posting Wanted Criminals

**‚úÖ DO:**

- **Verify Credentials:** Complete official verification process
- **Provide Case Numbers:** Include official case/warrant numbers
- **Mark Danger Levels:** Accurately indicate if armed and dangerous
- **Coordinate with Platform:** Work with our law enforcement liaison
- **Update Status:** Mark cases resolved when apprehended
- **Follow Protocol:** Adhere to department policies

**‚ùå DON'T:**

- **Post Unverified Cases:** Only post cases with active warrants
- **Endanger Public:** Clearly warn if subject is dangerous
- **Share Classified Info:** Don't share sensitive investigation details
- **Bypass Verification:** All law enforcement accounts must be verified

## For Finders/Tip Submitters

### ‚úÖ DO:

- **Submit Legitimate Tips:** Only share information you genuinely believe is helpful
- **Include Details:** Provide specific, actionable information
- **Add Evidence:** Include photos, videos, or location data when possible
- **Contact Law Enforcement:** For wanted criminals, call police first, then submit tip
- **Be Truthful:** Never fabricate or exaggerate information
- **Respect Privacy:** Don't share others' private information without consent
- **Follow Safety Warnings:** Heed "do not approach" warnings
- **Be Patient:** Allow time for verification
- **Communicate Clearly:** Provide clear, detailed descriptions

### ‚ùå DON'T:

- **Submit False Tips:** Never submit fabricated information for bounties
- **Approach Dangerous Individuals:** Do not confront armed or dangerous subjects
- **Trespass:** Don't enter private property illegally
- **Harass Subjects:** Don't contact or follow subjects excessively
- **Demand Payment:** Bounties are paid after verification, not before
- **Submit Duplicate Tips:** Don't spam the same tip repeatedly
- **Use Inappropriate Methods:** Don't obtain information illegally
- **Guarantee Results:** Don't promise outcomes you can't control

### Safety Rules for Finders

1. **Never Approach Armed/Dangerous Individuals**
   - Call 911 immediately if you spot a dangerous criminal
   - Submit tip to I Found!! after reporting to police
   - Stay at a safe distance

2. **Use Caution When Returning Lost Items**
   - Meet in public places
   - Bring a friend
   - Verify ownership before handing over items
   - Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, leave

3. **Protect Your Privacy**
   - Don't share your home address
   - Use in-app messaging when possible
   - Be cautious about sharing personal information

4. **Don't Trespass**
   - Stay on public property
   - Don't enter private property without permission
   - Follow all "No Trespassing" signs

## Reporting and Enforcement

### How to Report Violations

If you see content or behavior that violates these guidelines:

1. **Use the Report Button:** Available on all cases, tips, and profiles
2. **Provide Details:** Explain the violation clearly
3. **Include Evidence:** Screenshots if applicable
4. **Be Specific:** Cite which guideline was violated

**What Happens Next:**

- Our moderation team reviews reports within 24 hours
- Violating content is removed
- Repeat offenders may be suspended or banned
- Serious violations reported to law enforcement

### Consequences of Violations

**First Offense (Minor):**
- Warning
- Content removal
- Educational resources

**Second Offense or Moderate Violation:**
- Temporary suspension (7-30 days)
- Content removal
- Bounty privileges revoked temporarily

**Serious or Repeated Violations:**
- Permanent account termination
- Forfeiture of pending bounties
- Report to law enforcement (if illegal)
- Legal action (if warranted)

**Immediate Termination Offenses:**

- Submitting fraudulent tips for bounties
- Harassment or threats
- Posting illegal content
- Impersonating law enforcement
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Attempting to harm platform integrity

## Special Circumstances

### Missing Persons Cases

**Sensitivity Required:**
- These cases involve real families experiencing tragedy
- Comments must be respectful and helpful only
- No speculation about circumstances
- No victim-blaming
- Support families, don't judge

**What to Avoid:**
- "They probably just ran away"
- "Maybe they don't want to be found"
- Theories about foul play without evidence
- Comments about appearance or lifestyle
- Unsolicited advice to families

### Wanted Criminal Cases

**Safety is Critical:**
- NEVER attempt to apprehend yourself
- Call 911 for dangerous criminals
- Don't warn the subject
- Don't put yourself at risk for a bounty

**Legal Reminders:**
- Presume innocence until proven guilty
- Don't assume guilt based on accusations alone
- Respect legal process
- No vigilante justice

### Lost Pet Cases

**Special Considerations:**
- Owners are emotionally attached - be kind
- Verify microchips or tags before claiming bounties
- Don't hold pets for ransom (illegal)
- Contact animal control if pet appears injured or neglected

## Payment and Bounty Ethics

### For Posters:

**‚úÖ DO:**
- Set bounties you can actually afford
- Pay verified tips promptly
- Split bounties fairly if multiple tips contributed
- Be transparent about verification criteria

**‚ùå DON'T:**
- Promise bounties you can't pay
- Refuse to pay after verification
- Change bounty amounts after posting
- Dispute legitimate verifications

### For Finders:

**‚úÖ DO:**
- Submit tips to genuinely help
- Accept verification decisions gracefully
- Understand that payment requires verification

**‚ùå DON'T:**
- Submit tips only for money
- Harass posters about bounty payments
- Submit false information to collect bounties
- Attempt to extort higher bounties
